A Google initative, AngularJS remains as one of the most powerful and popular open-source web development frameworks currently available. 'Currently' is the operative term since it seems a html5/javascript framework is being developed every day! But until the day AngularJS is unseated as king, let us marvel at this wonderful set of tools. The site you are looking at is built entirely in AngularJS, but how does this impact clients? The simple reason is that AngularJS vastly simplifies and reduces the time between web-site conceptualization and final delivery without impacting creative design, user experience, and functionality. And most importantly, clients can now create powerful Single Page Applications (SPA) that allows them to take their business into the browser. With AngularJS you now have power to create flexibility, reliable, maintainable, compelling web applications. Now you can go anywhere your imagination takes you!
