JIRA (by Atlassian) provides bug tracking, issue tracking, and project management functions. No project can be successful without a process to monitor and assign all the tasks that go into a release. Developers (bless their hearts) HATE the administration of these tasks. But without a robust and comprehensive project management tool, failure looms large. In steps JIRA. With a click of a button JIRA allows you to share issues and search results. When you mention someone in JIRA, they will receive an email notification with a link to the issue.JIRA means transparency for all projects and tasks throughout the entire company. With JIRA, you won't forget any tasks that have gotten lost in email inboxes. With JIRA, it's always easy to figure out who was given what task to accomplish. You can see all information for a ticket on 1 screen: priority level, processing status, agent, and estimated time to completion. Are you dold on JIRA yet? I am!
