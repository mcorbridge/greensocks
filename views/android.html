Without getting into an acrimonious discussion about the merits of iOS versus android, or native vs hybrid, or the impact that ReactJS might have on the latter, let me just say that I enjoy developing androids apps. Feel free to conduct your own research under the headings: environment,configuration,UX design language, API's, sharing, fragmentation and publication. Maybe you will come to the same conclusion I did. Personally, I absolutely LOVE android studio (and it's price: FREE!) and the vast number of library's available to developers. While I can appreciate the user experience with iOS, I have to pay $100 just to proof-of-concept something on my own device?! And I need a Mac to develop xcode? I'll stick with android, thank you very much.
